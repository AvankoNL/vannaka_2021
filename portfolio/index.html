Animation

My unique selling point is tracing and animating characters from the online game Old School RuneScape. This way I know the community recognises my content, which strenghtens the Brand Awareness.

With the animations I want to give my viewers a quick taste of what's going to happen in the video. Sometimes this is done with a joke, but it can also be something epic or beautiful.

UI & UX

With my studies Communication & Multimedia Design I focus on improving User Interfaces and User Experience. (UI & UX). I've always enjoyed making guides, how-to's and tutorials. This is something I can translate to designing a (digital product) in a way that the user understands how it should be used.

YouTube Startup

Next to Content Creation I have worked as a freelancer to help companies with starting a YouTube channel. I read YouTube analytics, carry out market analysis and write strategy reports to give new channels kickstart in the world of YouTube.
